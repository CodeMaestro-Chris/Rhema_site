About Rhema University

Rhema University is committed to producing men and women who will be sound in learning, character- worthy, submissive to God’s sovereignty. Its founder, Living Word Ministries, under the leadership of Brother Emma Okorie, desires to run a first-class University that will be acclaimed worldwide.

Established in 2009, Rhema University Nigeria has for over a decade been at the forefront of advancing higher education in Nigeria. As captured in our motto, our focus is hinged on equipping humanity for excellence. We believe that educational/academic pursuit which does not prepare its graduates for life’s ever-increasing challenges is irrelevant to this age and time. We are proud to be recognized as an inspiring university with an excellent reputation for teaching, research, community service and moulding the spiritual lives of our students. We are committed to the transformative power of education guided by the principle of head-to-heart connection.

OUR LOCATION

Rhema University currently operates from its take-off site in 153-155 Aba-Owerri, Aba – one of Nigeria’s busiest commercial cities. The permanent site currently under construction is at Owerrinta, Abia State, Nigeria.

OUR MISSION

To produce leaders that will meet the challenges of development in this age of globalization and to cultivate the students’ profound professional expertise and high integrity.

PHILOSOPHY

Recognizing at God is the source of all knowledge and hinged on the philosophical dictum that knowledge is power: that giving man knowledge through education based on the fear of God will help man thrive and prosper in a globalized world. To achieve this philosophy, the University will pursue the highest standards of teaching, research, and community service while simultaneously providing a balanced education for the acquisition of knowledge, entrepreneurial skills, and moral principles.
